How it works

You provide evidence (your resume text). We create a structured profile, surface uncertainty, and help you reason about job decisions. The goal is better decisions — not embellishment.

1

Paste your resume

Text-only intake. We focus on roles, dates, skills, and seniority signals — not formatting.

2

Get a profile + readiness scores

We show what’s strong, what’s missing, and what’s uncertain — so you control the next steps.

3

Make decisions with a Decision Brief

When you’re ready, evaluate opportunities using evidence-backed fit, gaps, risks, and strategy.

What we won’t do
  • Invent experience, titles, or credentials
  • Produce “hireability” predictions
  • Hide uncertainty behind a single score
The employer is the final verifier. We focus on decision quality and evidence clarity.
